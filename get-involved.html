Get Involved with Jauneball

Organize a Tournament

Ready to bring the excitement of Jauneball to your community? Organizing a tournament is a great way to introduce people to the sport and build a local Jauneball community.

What You'll Need

  • A suitable playing field (approximately 30m x 20m)
  • At least 10-20 participants (5-10 per team)
  • Basic equipment (balls, cones, goals)
  • Volunteers to help organize and referee

Quick Start Guide

  1. Download our tournament organizer's guide below
  2. Find a suitable location and set a date
  3. Promote your event locally and online
  4. Gather equipment and volunteers
  5. Run your tournament and have fun!
